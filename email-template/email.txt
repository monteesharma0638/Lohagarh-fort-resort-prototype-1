Part 1: Mastering the Hook
 Here’s how to craft an opening that works:
Lead with clarity, not cleverness.
 Customers should know what’s inside at a glance. Mystery kills conversions.


Keep it short.
 Aim for 6–8 words in your subject line. Mobile inboxes cut the rest.
💡 Example: Subject: Your 10% discount ends tonight.
 First line: We don’t want you to miss out on saving before midnight.
Use action-driven language.
 Instead of “Update from Smith & Co.” try “Claim your free guide today.”


Pair with a strong first line.
 Your preview text should flow naturally from the subject. Think of them as partners.
